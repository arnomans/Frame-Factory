Services

Frame Factory specializes in custom framing and are leading experts in Photo Framing, Box Framing, canvas stretching as well as custom printing of framable materials.

Picture Framing

We specialize in picture framing, using top quality mouldings, different coloured mount boards and quality glass to frame photos, artworks, paintings, mirrors and much more.

Box Framing

Box framing can be used to frame almost any item thinkable, and consists of a manufactured box to contain the item to be framed. We produce box frames custom to the product you want framed.

Printing

We also offer an exclusive printing services to print directly on Canvas, printing of photo graphic paper for framing as well as PVC prints to bring your image and photos to life.
